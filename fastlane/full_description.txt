Application to discover historic events with 4 diferents pages:

- Home

This page will show you the historic event of the day

- Search

This page will show all the historic events that are in the database and you can also filter them
You can click on the eye to check the time line with the same historic events
You can also add a favorite and click on an event to show his details and his pictures

- Favorites

Show all your favorite events

- Quiz

Will ask you questions about some historic events
